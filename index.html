Types of Non-Bank Lending Solutions

  1. Peer-to-Peer Lending Platforms - Direct lending between individuals and businesses through online marketplaces

  2. Crowdfunding Campaigns - Community-driven capital raising for startups and innovative projects

Peer-to-peer lending platforms operate as digital marketplaces where investors directly fund business loans, bypassing traditional banking intermediaries. Revenue-based financing allows businesses to tap into future sales for immediate capital without surrendering equity. Companies receive upfront funding in exchange for a percentage of future revenues until a predetermined amount is repaid. This structure makes it ideal for businesses with predictable revenue streams but seasonal fluctuations.

Q: What is the $100,000 loophole for family loans?

The “$100,000 loophole” refers to specific IRS rules for intra-family loans under $100,000, where no interest needs to be imputed if the borrower’s net investment income does not exceed $1,000. This provision helps families avoid certain taxable gift implications.
